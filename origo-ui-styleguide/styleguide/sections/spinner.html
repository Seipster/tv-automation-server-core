Spinner

Spinner-animations to show while loading important stuff. cx, cy and r of the circle-element of the svg will sadly have to correspond with the CSS-size set. Ex. for a large (30px) spinner, cx and cy will have to be radius+stroke-max (12), and r the radius (10).
