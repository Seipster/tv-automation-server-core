Pills occupy more vertical space than regular paragraphs. For this reason, pills will not stack neatly on multiple lines by default. If you need a bunch of pills that may or may not flow on multiple lines, add the pills class to the container element. This will give the pills more vertical spacing, but now they may no longer align neatly with text across columns.
